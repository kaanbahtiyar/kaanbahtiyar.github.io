Publications

Also on Google Scholar. Titles link to the journal or proceedings; each entry links to the project it belongs to.

Journal Articles

  • Data-driven adaptive compensation of tool runout in milling via machine tool feed drives
    ASME Journal of Manufacturing Science and Engineering · 2026

    A data-driven adaptive strategy that uses the machine's own feed drives to compensate milling tool runout/eccentricity in real time from force/vibration feedback, formulated as a convex optimization.

    Tool eccentricity compensation
  • Adaptive tool eccentricity compensation using machine tool feed drives
    MM Science Journal (HSM2025 Special Issue) · 2025

    On-machine eccentricity cancellation that commands spindle-frequency circular motion to the feed axes, tuned by an extremum-seeking controller from cutting-force feedback.

    Tool eccentricity compensation
  • Model-free optimal spindle speed control for real-time vibration mitigation in milling
    CIRP Journal of Manufacturing Science and Technology · 2025

    A model-free extremum-seeking spindle-speed controller that minimizes overall machining vibration from real-time data, with stability analysis and tuning guidance, validated against a conventional approach.

    Spindle speed control
  • Data-driven feedforward control of inertial dampers for accuracy improvement
    CIRP Annals · 2024

    A pre-scheduled feedforward signal, learned over repeated tasks within actuator stroke/force limits, that assists an inertial damper's feedback loop for up to 87% lower peak vibration.

    Active inertial damper control
  • Lightweight and robust chatter detection algorithms for milling
    Manufacturing Letters (NAMRC 50 Special Issue) · 2022

    Two chatter detectors: a computationally efficient spectral-power method and a more robust PCA-based method, both using a power ratio above 0.5 to flag chatter, tested on flexible workpieces.

    Chatter detection

Conference Papers, Peer-reviewed (proceedings)

  • Brief paper: Model-free adaptive spindle speed regulation for mitigation of machining vibrations
    ASME MSEC 2024 · 2024

    Two model-free spindle-speed strategies, a dither-based search and a dither-free least-squares approach, that find chatter-free conditions and reduce forced vibration from in-situ measurements.

    Spindle speed control

Conference Papers, Peer-reviewed (no proceedings)

  • Adaptive spindle speed control for real-time machining vibration mitigation
    LEM21 (11th Int. Conf. on Leading Edge Manufacturing in 21st Century) · 2025

    An extremum-seeking spindle-speed controller that regulates speed in real time from accelerometer data, with a data-driven least-squares gradient, outperforming conventional regulation.

    Spindle speed control
  • Compensation of axis-coupled inertial forced vibrations using machine tool feed drives
    ICPE2024 (20th Int. Conf. on Precision Engineering) · 2024

    A trajectory pre-filter that commands the Z-axis to counteract X/Y-coupled vibration in a vertical machining center, tuned iteratively from data, lowering coupled vibration about 80%.

    Cross-coupled vibration compensation

Conference Papers, Abstract-reviewed

  • Data-driven feedforward control of active inertial dampers for high-performance vibration mitigation
    39th Annual Meeting of the ASPE · 2024

    Iterative learning control that shapes a pre-scheduled feedforward signal for an inertial actuator to eliminate transient vibration in ultra-precision machine tools within actuator limits.

    Active inertial damper control
  • Extremum seeking control based real-time optimization of spindle speed in machining
    37th Annual Meeting of the ASPE · 2022

    An extremum-seeking spindle-speed optimizer that minimizes a total-vibration cost (forced plus chatter) from measurements, with no process model or stability lobes.

    Spindle speed control
  • Time-domain based early detection of chatter vibrations
    36th Annual Meeting of the ASPE · 2021

    A time-domain detector that separates cutting and chatter components with a Kalman filter and an energy-ratio metric, flagging chatter onset earlier than frequency-domain methods.

    Chatter detection
